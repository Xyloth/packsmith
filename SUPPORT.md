# Support

Email: founder@xyflowinnovations.com

PackSmith is safe-by-default:

- scans are read-only;
- fixes require preview;
- destructive cleanup is not part of v1;
- reports are designed to make bug reports reproducible.

## Good Bug Reports

Include:

- PackSmith version;
- Unity version;
- render pipeline;
- scan target type;
- exported Markdown or JSON report;
- reproduction steps;
- screenshots if the UI is involved.

## Suggestions

Suggestions are welcome when they make PackSmith easier, safer, faster, broader, or more useful for asset-pack intake.
